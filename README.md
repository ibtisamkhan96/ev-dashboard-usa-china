# EV Wars: USA vs China — 2025 Market Dashboard

Interactive dashboard comparing the top 5 EV makers in the US (Tesla, GM, Ford, Rivian, Lucid) and China (BYD, Geely, XPeng, Li Auto, NIO) on full-year 2025 deliveries, growth, and market share.

**Live demo:** see the Netlify deployment linked in the repo sidebar.

## Highlights

- China's NEV market hit 15.33M units in 2025 (+25%); the US fell to 1.28M (−2%), its first decline
- BYD delivered ~4.6M vehicles, 3.5x the entire US EV market
- XPeng grew 126%, Geely's NEV arm 81%, GM 48%; Tesla slipped 8.6%
- Tesla holds 46% of the US market; BYD leads China with 27.2%

## Stack

Single HTML file. Chart.js 4 for visuals, no build step. Tabs, country filters, log-scale comparisons, dark theme.

## Data sources

Company filings and delivery releases (FY2025), CleanTechnica, CnEVPost, Electrek, CAAM/CPCA via Global Times, Yahoo Finance.
